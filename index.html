About

This sandbox is a controlled environment where interactive and immersive technologies can be tested, broken, and reassembled without consequence. Built through vibe coding: an intuitive, improvisational approach to making where curiosity leads and outcomes follow. Many of the projects here have no immediate purpose beyond the experience of discovering what happens when technology is given room to play. They exist to probe, to test, to ask what if. Because imagination needs space before it needs purpose - and the most interesting discoveries tend to live just past the point where the plan ran out. Potential rarely arrives fully formed. It surfaces through experimentation - through the willingness to begin without knowing the end. - Chris Shaw
